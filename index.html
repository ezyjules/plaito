Snap a photo of a question, highlight it, and work through it together — any subject, any year level, any school system. Points for every question you finish — streaks for getting them right.

⚙️ Let's get set up — ready for class

This app has no company behind it — no accounts, no server, nothing collected. Everything it needs lives right here on your device, which is why we set it up once at the top. Takes about two minutes, then it folds away.

1 · Pick a look
2 · Connect an AI key

The tutoring brain is rented directly from an AI company — that's what the key is for. Think of it like a prepaid SIM card for AI: you load a little credit with Claude or ChatGPT, and each question sips about a cent of it. $5–10 of credit typically lasts a whole school term. No subscription, and you can see exactly what's been spent on the provider's site.

Claude keys: console.anthropic.com → API Keys. ChatGPT keys: platform.openai.com → API keys. The key stays in this browser, is only ever sent to that provider, and stays saved here until you clear browsing data.

3 · Pick a voice

The tutor reads questions and explanations aloud. The voices in this list come free with your device — quality varies, so hit ▶ Test and pick the least robotic. (Mac tip: System Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Manage Voices lets you download much nicer "Premium" ones for free — they'll appear here.)

Want a properly human voice? ElevenLabs is a company that makes startlingly natural AI voices. It's optional: sign up free at elevenlabs.io (the free tier covers ~10 minutes of speech a month), grab your API key from your profile, and paste it here — starred ⭐ voices will join the list above.

4 · Your class (optional)

Country, year level, school system, current topic — shapes terminology, methods, and practice difficulty. Leave blank and the tutor adapts to each photo.

On a phone or iPad this opens the camera straight away — point, shoot, done. On the Mac it opens a picker: in Safari choose Take Photo to snap with your iPhone, or pick an existing photo.
